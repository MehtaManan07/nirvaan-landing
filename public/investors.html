We are creating Nirvaan - a Web3 based career platform for college students, professionals, and corporations that rewards each stakeholder who adds value to the group. Value can be added by guiding the community, providing data, and developing content & tools.

One platform to rule them all.

Nirvaan is designed to provide a single platform for all your career related pursuits - exploration, upskilling, and connecting with opportunities.

DAO

Built by humans.
Driven by technology.

The endeavor we are on requires active participation of the users. It is they who add value to the platform by contributing to the community knowledge base. It is natural that they should be rewarded as the platform grows with both governance and ownership.

To this end, the platform will gradually grow into a decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) owned and governed by its community members.
